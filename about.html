About

Hello again! I'm Ellah, an aspiring designer and artist based in Boston, MA. I graduated with a BS in Biochemistry and minor in Studio Art from Tufts University. I've always been passionate about art and design, it is the place where I truly feel like myself. I've also always been very curious and interested in puzzles and reactions and mechanisms and just how things worked so I went down the science path thinking that was th perfect place to find that, until I discovered web development and programming one day when I was bored. It seemed like the perfect combination of my artistic side as well as my love of seeing how things connect to create something meaningful.

Among the many things I love and enjoy are watercolor painting, movies, music, Marvel and comics, spending hours on Youtube learning random skills, and dreaming about opening a coffee shop in the middle of New York or London or working on the next big Marvel movie.

Currently looking for opportunities to grow my skills and be a part of something impactful. Feel free to reach me through LinkedIn or by email at ellahnzikoba@gmail.com.

Thank you!
